The pioneers

From Antiquity to today, countless women have shaped science, yet their stories often remain unknown.

Scientific and historical literature brings these exceptional journeys back to life. Discover biographies, essays, and narratives celebrating women in science, their discoveries, and the obstacles they overcame to advance human knowledge.

Give a voice to women erased from history and explore their fascinating journeys through this compelling essay. An anthology tracing the lives of women who shaped science despite the odds. The true stories of African-American women who made NASA’s greatest achievements possible.
